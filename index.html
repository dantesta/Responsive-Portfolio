About Me

I am excited to continue my education in this bootcamp. I took a few coding classes (HTML, CSS, JS) during college, but I still consider myself very new to coding. I would love to be able to walk away from this bootcamp with the ability to develop an application. I have a creative mind and I want to merge that with the technical skills of coding to make myself more well rounded.
